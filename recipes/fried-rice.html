Fried Rice

Description

A classic stir-fried rice dish made with day-old rice, scrambled eggs, carrots, green peas, garlic, and spring onions. Seasoned with soy sauce and black pepper, it delivers a savory flavor with fluffy grains of rice and colorful vegetables in every bite.

This dish is perfect for using leftover rice and can be easily customized with your favorite ingredients. It is commonly served as a main dish or as a side with other meals.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cold cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup diced carrots
  • ¼ cup green peas
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Scramble the eggs until cooked, then remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced carrots and cook for about 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the green peas and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the cold cooked rice and break apart any clumps.
  7. Pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce, then season with black pepper.
  8. Stir-fry until the rice is evenly coated with the seasonings.
  9. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix well.
  10. Add the chopped spring onions, stir for another minute, and serve hot.
