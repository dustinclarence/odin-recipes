Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce.

It is baked until the cheese becomes golden brown and is often served as a hearty family meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake until golden brown.
  5. Let cool before serving.
